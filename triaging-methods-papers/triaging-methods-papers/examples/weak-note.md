# Weak Example (Anti-Pattern)

This paper proves adaptive interfaces work well for all students in most contexts.

The methods were good and the sample looked large enough.

I would immediately use this design in my own work.

## Why this is weak

- No clear research question.
- No explicit design, population, measures, or analysis details.
- Claims are not grounded with quotes or page/section pointers.
- Overgeneralized conclusions with no stated limitations.
- No reusable methods decomposition.
- Missing `Not reported` markers for absent details.

## Fixes required

- Replace with the full template from `templates/paper-method-note.md`.
- Add evidence table entries for every major methods claim.
- Separate what is observed from what is interpretation.
- Add validity threats, replicability signals, and reuse boundaries.
