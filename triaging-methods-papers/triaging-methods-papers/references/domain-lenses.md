# Domain Lenses for Methods Extraction

Apply these prompts after core extraction to improve method relevance.

## Educational Psychology

- What learner constructs are central (motivation, self-efficacy, identity, metacognition)?
- Are interventions and comparison conditions described clearly enough to reuse?
- Are measurement instruments validated for the target age/population?
- Are classroom constraints (time, teacher workload, curriculum fit) reported?

## Educational Data Mining (EDM)

- What is the unit of analysis (student, session, utterance, assignment, class)?
- Are feature engineering and label definitions clear?
- Is data leakage risk addressed?
- Are evaluation metrics and baselines appropriate and transparent?
- Is there enough pipeline detail to reproduce preprocessing/modeling?

## Human-Computer Interaction (HCI)

- Is task design ecologically valid relative to real use?
- Are interaction logs, usability metrics, and qualitative protocols clearly defined?
- Are participant recruitment and context likely to bias findings?
- Are interface elements/prototypes documented enough for reuse?

## Cognitive Science

- Are constructs and manipulations theoretically coherent?
- Is the design appropriate for the claimed mechanism?
- Are alternative explanations and boundary conditions addressed?
- Do results likely transfer beyond controlled settings?

## Learning Research / Learning Sciences

- Is learning context sufficiently described (setting, duration, facilitation model)?
- Are socio-cultural factors or implementation fidelity captured?
- Are outcomes measured at meaningful timescales?
- Are mechanisms for learning gains plausible and evidenced?

## Cross-Domain Synthesis Prompts

Use these for `Method Reuse Potential` and `Comparison Hooks`:

- Which parts are directly reusable vs context-specific?
- Which design choices should be copied, adapted, or avoided?
- What would be the minimum viable replication design?
- What paper types would best complement this one (contrasting method, larger sample, different population)?
